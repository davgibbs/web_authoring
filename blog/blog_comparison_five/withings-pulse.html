Withings Pulse

Released by French company Withings in June 2013, Withings Pulse is a fitness tracker that helps monitor items like steps taken, elevation climbed, calories burned, heart rate, and sleep duration. The heart rate function works by sliding your finger over the sensor that is located on the back of the Pulse device. The data is gathered and synced to your smartphone via Bluetooth 4.0. You can view your data on your Android or iOS devices using a free application called Withings Health Mate.


With a battery life of two weeks, this device can be worn on your wrist or else clipped onto your trousers, which may provide more accurate data.
