Basis B1

Realesed by Basis, an Intel company, in mid 2013, the Basis B1 band combines the best of activity and sleep tracking in the convenient guise of a wristwatch. As with other shower-proof wrist activity trackers, the benefit of this approach is its 24/7 wearability. Web applications are available for Android and iOS, and the device is synced wirelessly.


The Basis B1 fitness tracker makes use of five different sensors including of skin contact and an optical sensor to collect data such as heart rate and perspiration data.
